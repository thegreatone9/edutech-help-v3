What can you do with Edutech?


Edutech is a knowledge and skill sharing platform. If you are a teacher or a mentor, Edutech allows you to share your skills and monetize the teaching process. Introduce your students to a seamless classroom to digial learning experience. For teachers who are already conducting classes, Edutech can allow you to share their teaching sessions, with total control over them, with students outside the classroom, outside the building, outside the city, wherever! As a teacher, you can monitor their progress and extend your support truly beyond the classroom with our One-Click Digital Streaming Technology. On the flip side, if you are a seeker of knowledge, Edutech enables you to attend and interact with your teachers online, just like a traditional classroom. With a functional, elegant design proving that an easy to use interface doesn't just have to be minimal. You are also provided with an array of useful features that will leave you wondering how you ever managed to get along without Edutech. Sneak a peak of some of them below!
